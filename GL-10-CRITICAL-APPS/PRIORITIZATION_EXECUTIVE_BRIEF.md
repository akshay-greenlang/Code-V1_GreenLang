- **Integration risk:** Proven ERP patterns from GL-VCCI

- **Scalability risk:** Multi-tenant architecture proven at scale

- **Revenue visibility:** Regulatory deadlines create predictable demand
- **Portfolio risk:** 10 apps across different regulations reduce single-app dependency

## Decision Framework: Why This Order?

### Primary Factor: Infrastructure Alignment (70%)
Applications that leverage existing infrastructure launch 40-45% faster. SB 253 and EUDR should launch first because they reuse 78-80% of existing code.

### Secondary Factor: Revenue Potential (20%)
Building BPS ranks 5th (not 3rd) despite only 72% reuse because $150M revenue justifies complexity. Product PCF ranks last despite 60% reuse because $200M revenue is highest.

### Tertiary Factor: Deadline Pressure (10%)
EUDR has Dec 2025 deadline but gets 6-month extension grace. SB 253 has 18 months. Neither are hard constraints (unlike CSRD which launched Dec 2025). Regulatory dates are less important than infrastructure readiness.
