## Executive Summary

GreenLang Packs represent pre-configured, industry-specific bundles of agents, tools, and configurations that deliver complete solutions for sustainability challenges. Each pack leverages 5-50 agents from our 10,000+ agent taxonomy to create turnkey solutions that can be deployed in days rather than months.

- **Total Addressable Market:** $250B+ across all pack categories
- **Average Pack Price:** $25,000/year (range: $500-$500,000)
- **Implementation Time:** 1-30 days (vs. 3-12 months custom)
- **ROI:** 10-50x within first year
- **License Models:** Subscription (70%), Usage-based (20%), Perpetual (10%)
