## Executive Summary

This roadmap outlines GreenLang's transformation from a startup with 84 planned agents to the world's leading Climate Intelligence AI platform with 10,000+ agents, 500+ applications, and 1000+ packs by 2030. The Agent Factory technology enables 200× faster development, positioning GreenLang to capture a significant share of the $50B+ climate tech market.

**"By 2030, GreenLang will be the global standard for Climate Intelligence AI, powering sustainability compliance and decision-making for 10,000+ enterprises worldwide through our comprehensive agent ecosystem."**

- **Revenue:** $2M → $1B+ ARR (500× growth)
