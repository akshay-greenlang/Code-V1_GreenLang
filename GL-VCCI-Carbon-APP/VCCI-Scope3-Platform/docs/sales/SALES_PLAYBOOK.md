- What's your timeline for CSRD/CDP/other reporting?

- Who has budget authority for this investment?
- What's your budget range for carbon accounting technology?
- Is this an OpEx or CapEx purchase?
- When does your fiscal year start? When are budgets approved?

**D = Decision Criteria**
- What factors will you use to evaluate carbon accounting platforms?
- How are you weighing price vs. capabilities vs. time-to-value?
- What's more important: automation or flexibility/customization?
