## KEY FINDINGS BY APPLICATION

### 1. EUDR (EU Deforestation Regulation)
**CRITICAL URGENCY - 50 DAYS TO COMPLIANCE**

**Key Requirements:**
- Geolocation data with 6 decimal precision for all plots >4 hectares
- Complete chain of custody documentation
- Risk assessment scoring system
- Due diligence statement submission before market placement

**Technical Needs:**
- Satellite monitoring integration
- Polygon mapping capabilities
- Supply chain traceability platform
- API connection to EU Information System

**Scope:**
- All companies trading in cattle, cocoa, coffee, palm oil, rubber, soy, wood
- SMEs have until Jun 30, 2025 (extended deadline)

---

### 2. California SB 253 (Climate Disclosure)
**HIGH PRIORITY - 7 MONTHS TO FIRST REPORTING**

**Key Requirements:**
- Scope 1 & 2 emissions by 2026 (2025 data)
- Scope 3 emissions by 2027 (all 15 categories)
- Third-party verification required
- GHG Protocol compliant calculations

**Technical Needs:**
- Emissions calculation engine
- Supplier data collection portal
- CARB reporting format compliance
- Verification workflow system

**Scope:**
- Companies with >$1 billion revenue doing business in California
- Consolidated reporting allowed for parent companies

---

### 3. Green Claims Directive
**MEDIUM PRIORITY - 10 MONTHS TO COMPLIANCE**

**Key Requirements:**
- Scientific substantiation for ALL environmental claims
- Life cycle assessment where relevant
- QR codes linking to full evidence
- Pre-market verification for large companies

**Technical Needs:**
- LCA software integration
- Claims management database
- QR code generation system
- Multi-language support

**Scope:**
- All companies making environmental claims in EU
- Micro-enterprises exempt (unless comparative claims)

---

### 4. EU Taxonomy (Financial Institutions)
**HIGH PRIORITY - 2 MONTHS TO ENHANCED REPORTING**

**Key Requirements:**
- Green Asset Ratio (GAR) calculation
- Taxonomy alignment assessment
- Technical screening criteria evaluation
- DNSH and minimum safeguards compliance

**Technical Needs:**
- Portfolio analytics platform
- NACE code mapping system
- TSC assessment engine
- Look-through calculation for funds

**Scope:**
- All banks, insurers, asset managers
- Large corporates (CSRD scope)

---

### 5. Building Performance Standards
**ROLLING DEADLINES - JURISDICTION SPECIFIC**

**Key Requirements:**
- Energy/emissions benchmarking
- Performance improvements to targets
- Annual reporting via ENERGY STAR
- Retrofit planning and execution

**Technical Needs:**
- Energy monitoring platform
- Building analytics system
- Compliance tracking dashboard
- M&V capabilities

**Scope:**
- Generally buildings >25,000 sq ft
- Varies by jurisdiction

---

### 6. CSDDD (Corporate Due Diligence)
**LONG-TERM - 20 MONTHS TO PREPARE**

**Key Requirements:**
- Human rights and environmental due diligence
- Value chain impact assessment
- Climate transition plan (1.5°C aligned)
- Stakeholder engagement and grievance mechanisms

**Technical Needs:**
- Supply chain risk platform
- Impact monitoring system
- Stakeholder portal
- Remediation tracking

**Scope:**
- Phase 1 (2027): >5,000 employees & >€1.5B turnover
- Phase 2 (2028): >3,000 employees & >€900M turnover
- Phase 3 (2029): >1,000 employees & >€450M turnover

---

### 7. Digital Product Passport & PCF
**BATTERY FOCUS - 15 MONTHS TO BATTERY PASSPORT**

**Key Requirements:**
- Unique identifier and QR code
- Carbon footprint declaration
- Material composition disclosure
- Performance and durability data
- Supply chain due diligence info

**Technical Needs:**
- Product database system
- Carbon calculation engine
- QR code management platform
- API for data access
- Distributed ledger integration

**Scope:**
- EV and industrial batteries >2kWh (Feb 2027)
- Extending to textiles, electronics, construction products
