**Product Code:** GL-006 | **Version:** 2.0 | **Category:** Heat Recovery Optimization Platform

## Product Overview

WasteHeatRecovery is an AI-powered platform for identifying, optimizing, and managing waste heat recovery across industrial operations. Capture 30-60% of previously wasted thermal energy, reducing fuel costs by 10-25% and achieving measurable carbon reductions.

### Supported Technologies

**High Temperature (>400C):**
- Waste heat boilers
- Air preheaters
- Regenerators
- Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC)

**Medium Temperature (150-400C):**
- Economizers
- Shell & tube exchangers
- Heat pipes
- Absorption chillers

**Low Temperature (<150C):**
- Plate heat exchangers
- Heat pumps
- Run-around coils
- Condensate recovery
