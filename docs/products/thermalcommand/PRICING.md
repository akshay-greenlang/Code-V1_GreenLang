## 7. Performance-Based Pricing

### Gain-Share Model

For qualified customers, we offer performance-based pricing where fees are tied to verified energy savings:

| Component | Structure |
|-----------|-----------|
| Base Fee | 50% of standard subscription |
| Performance Fee | 20% of verified annual savings |
| Minimum Savings Guarantee | 10% energy reduction |
| Measurement | Third-party verified M&V |

**Example:**
- Standard pricing: $20,000/month = $240,000/year
- Gain-share pricing: $10,000/month base = $120,000/year base
- If savings = $1M: Performance fee = $200,000
- Total: $320,000 (vs. $240,000 standard)
- If savings = $500K: Performance fee = $100,000
- Total: $220,000 (vs. $240,000 standard)

*Gain-share available for 3-year commitments with minimum $500K annual thermal energy spend*
