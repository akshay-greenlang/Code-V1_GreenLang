## Core Capabilities

### Real-Time Monitoring
- All pollutant categories (GHG, criteria, HAPs, VOCs)
- Direct CEMS, predictive CEMS, parametric methods
- Real-time permit limit tracking
- Mobile dashboard access
- Multi-facility visibility

### Predictive Compliance
- 4-72 hour exceedance prediction (95% accuracy)
- Rolling limit tracking with margin analysis
- Operational impact forecasting
- Weather-adjusted dispersion modeling
- Proactive alert notifications

### Automated Reporting
- EPA Part 75, Part 98, NESHAP, NSPS
- State air permits (all 50 states)
- EU ETS, RGGI, California Cap-and-Trade
- GHG Protocol Scope 1/2/3
- CDP, TCFD, SEC climate disclosures

### Carbon Management
- Scope 1, 2, 3 inventory tracking
- Allowance/credit portfolio management
- Internal carbon pricing implementation
- Reduction pathway modeling
- Offset quality verification
