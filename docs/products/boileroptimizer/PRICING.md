## 7. Performance-Based Pricing

### Gain-Share Option

For qualified customers, we offer performance-based pricing:

| Component | Structure |
|-----------|-----------|
| Base Fee | 60% of standard subscription |
| Performance Fee | 25% of verified fuel savings |
| Minimum Guarantee | 8% fuel reduction |
| Measurement Period | Annual with quarterly reviews |
| Verification | Third-party or agreed methodology |

**Example - 5 Boilers, $500K Annual Fuel Spend:**
- Standard pricing: $6,000/month = $72,000/year
- Gain-share base: $43,200/year (60%)
- If savings = 12% ($60K): Performance fee = $15,000
- Total: $58,200 (vs. $72,000 standard)

**Eligibility:**
- Minimum $250K annual fuel spend
- 3-year minimum commitment
- Baseline measurement period required

## 8. Pricing Calculator

### Quick Estimate Formula

```
Monthly Cost = Platform Fee + (Boilers x Per-Boiler Fee) x (1 - Volume Discount)

Year 1 Total = (Monthly Cost x 12) + Implementation + Hardware
```
