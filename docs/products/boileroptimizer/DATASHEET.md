## Product Overview

BoilerOptimizer is an AI-powered boiler management platform that delivers real-time combustion optimization, predictive maintenance, and emissions compliance for industrial steam and hot water boilers. Reduce fuel costs by 8-15% while improving reliability and meeting regulatory requirements.

## Core Capabilities

### Combustion Optimization
- Real-time O2 trim control (3-6% fuel savings)
- CO monitoring for complete combustion
- Automated burner tuning recommendations
- Multi-fuel support (gas, oil, biogas)
- Air-fuel ratio optimization with safety interlocks

### Efficiency Monitoring
- ASME PTC 4.1 efficiency calculations
- Continuous heat loss analysis (stack, blowdown, radiation)
- Comparative efficiency trending
- Fuel quality normalization
- Real-time cost-per-steam calculations

### Predictive Maintenance
- Tube failure prediction (30-90 days advance)
- Refractory wear monitoring
- Burner fouling detection
- Fan/motor health analysis
- Water-side corrosion indicators

### Emissions Compliance
- Continuous NOx, CO, CO2 tracking
- EPA NESHAP Subpart DDDDD support
- Automated regulatory reporting
- Permit limit monitoring with alerts
- Audit-ready documentation

### Multi-Boiler Management
- Lead-lag sequencing optimization
- Efficiency-based load allocation
- Standby boiler management
- Peak demand reduction
- Seasonal scheduling
