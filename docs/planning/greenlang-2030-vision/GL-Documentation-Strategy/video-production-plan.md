# GreenLang Video Production Plan 2025-2030

## Executive Summary

Our video content strategy aims to create 500+ hours of educational content by 2030, establishing GreenLang as the premier resource for sustainability technology education through visual learning.

## Content Guidelines

### Video Format Standards
- Resolution: 4K (3840×2160)
- Frame rate: 30/60 fps
- Audio: 48kHz, stereo
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Captions: SRT files required

### Branding Guidelines
- Intro/outro: 5 seconds each
- Logo placement: Top right
- Color scheme: GreenLang brand colors
- Font: Consistent typography
- Thumbnail template: Standardized

### Accessibility Requirements
- Closed captions: All videos
- Audio descriptions: Where applicable
- Transcripts: Available for download
- Sign language: Major announcements
- Multiple playback speeds: Supported

## Risk Mitigation

### Content Risks
- Technical inaccuracies: Expert review process
- Outdated content: Regular update cycles
- Platform changes: Multi-platform strategy
- Copyright issues: Original content only

### Production Risks
- Team availability: Buffer content
- Equipment failure: Backup systems
- Budget constraints: Phased approach
- Quality issues: QA processes

### Distribution Risks
- Platform policy changes: Diversification
- Algorithm changes: Multiple channels
- Bandwidth costs: CDN optimization
- Regional restrictions: Localization
