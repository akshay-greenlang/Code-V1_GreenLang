## What Enterprise tier unlocks

### Security + compliance
- **SSO + SCIM** — Okta, Azure AD, Ping, Google Workspace out of the box
- **VPC deployment** — private AWS / Azure / GCP subnet; your data
  never transits the public API
- **Private registry** — your methodologies, your factors, your vault
- **Signed releases** — every edition of the catalog is Ed25519 signed;
  your auditors can verify exactly which version you queried
- **SOC 2 Type II** — annual audit, evidence package delivered on
  request
- **ISO 27001** — management system certified
- **GDPR + CCPA compliant** — DPA available, DSR endpoints, regional
  data residency

### Governance
- **Approval workflows** — multi-party review on factor overrides
- **Change management** — audit trail on every methodology tweak
- **Segregation of duties** — separate accounts for analysts, reviewers,
  approvers, auditors
- **Regulatory mapping** — every factor tagged against CSRD, CDP, TCFD,
  ISSB S2, CBAM

### Data rights
- **All 5 redistribution classes** supported including `oem_redistributable`
- **Unlimited private overrides** — bring your own methodology
- **Unlimited sub-tenants** — for holding companies / subsidiaries
- **Customer-private factors** — your competitive intelligence stays
  yours

### Reliability
- **99.95% uptime SLA** with credit schedule
- **24/7 on-call support** + named Technical Account Manager
- **RTO / RPO commitments** in the MSA
- **Disaster recovery** — multi-region active-passive, documented
