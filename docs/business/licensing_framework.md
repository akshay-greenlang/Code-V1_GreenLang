## Executive Summary

This document establishes the comprehensive licensing framework for GreenLang products, including license types, terms and conditions, compliance requirements, and audit provisions. This framework ensures clear rights and obligations for both GreenLang and its customers while protecting intellectual property and enabling flexible deployment options.

## 1. License Types

### 1.1 License Type Overview

| License Type | Description | Billing Model | Term | Best For |
|--------------|-------------|---------------|------|----------|
| **Subscription** | Ongoing access with continuous updates | Annual/Monthly | 1-5 years | Most customers |
| **Perpetual** | One-time purchase with optional maintenance | Upfront + Maintenance | Perpetual | Regulated industries |
| **Usage-Based** | Pay per consumption | Per transaction/API call | Ongoing | Variable workloads |
| **Enterprise Agreement** | Custom terms for large deployments | Negotiated | Multi-year | Fortune 500 |
| **OEM/Embedded** | Licensing for integration into third-party products | Per unit or revenue share | Varies | Partners |

### 1.2 Subscription License

#### 1.2.1 Definition

A subscription license grants the customer the right to use GreenLang software for a defined period, with access to all updates, upgrades, and support included in the subscription tier.

#### 1.2.2 Subscription Terms

| Term Length | Payment Options | Discount | Auto-Renewal |
|-------------|-----------------|----------|--------------|
| Monthly | Monthly in advance | +20% premium | Yes (30-day notice to cancel) |
| Annual | Annual in advance | List price | Yes (60-day notice to cancel) |
| 2-Year | Annual in advance | 8-10% discount | Yes (90-day notice to cancel) |
| 3-Year | Annual or upfront | 12-15% discount | No (renewal negotiation) |
| 5-Year | Upfront or annual | 15-20% discount | No (renewal negotiation) |

#### 1.2.3 Subscription Rights

**Included:**
- Use of licensed software during subscription term
- Access to all updates and new versions released during term
- Support services per subscription tier
- Data storage per tier limits
- API access per tier limits

**Not Included:**
- Perpetual rights (access ends at subscription termination)
- Source code access
- Resale or redistribution rights
- Modification of core platform

### 1.3 Perpetual License

#### 1.3.1 Definition

A perpetual license grants the customer an indefinite right to use a specific version of GreenLang software, subject to the terms of the license agreement.

#### 1.3.2 Perpetual License Structure

| Component | Description | Typical Cost |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| **License Fee** | One-time perpetual license purchase | 2.5x annual subscription |
| **First Year Maintenance** | Updates, upgrades, support | Included |
| **Annual Maintenance** | Ongoing updates and support | 20% of license fee |
| **Professional Services** | Implementation (required) | $50,000 - $200,000 |

#### 1.3.3 Perpetual License Rights

**Included:**
- Indefinite use of licensed version
- Major and minor updates during maintenance
- Support during active maintenance
- Data export at any time

**Not Included:**
- New versions beyond maintenance period
- Cloud hosting (requires separate agreement)
- Support after maintenance lapses
- Upgrades to new major versions without maintenance

#### 1.3.4 Perpetual License Pricing

| Edition | Subscription (Annual) | Perpetual License | Annual Maintenance |
|---------|----------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Essentials | $100,000 | $250,000 | $50,000 (20%) |
| Professional | $250,000 | $625,000 | $125,000 (20%) |
| Enterprise | $500,000 | $1,250,000 | $250,000 (20%) |

### 1.4 Usage-Based License

#### 1.4.1 Definition

A usage-based license allows customers to pay based on actual consumption of GreenLang services, ideal for variable workloads or pilot deployments.

#### 1.4.2 Usage Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Unit Price |
|--------|------------|------------|
| **Data Points Processed** | Individual sensor/data readings | $0.001 per point |
| **API Calls** | REST API requests | $0.01 per 1,000 calls |
| **Agent Invocations** | AI agent executions | $0.10 per invocation |
| **Reports Generated** | Compliance/analytics reports | $5.00 per report |
| **Users (MAU)** | Monthly active users | $50 per MAU |

#### 1.4.3 Usage-Based Pricing Tiers

| Monthly Usage | Base Fee | Rate Discount |
|---------------|----------|---------------|
| $0 - $5,000 | $500 | List price |
| $5,001 - $10,000 | $500 | 10% off |
| $10,001 - $25,000 | $500 | 15% off |
| $25,001 - $50,000 | $500 | 20% off |
| $50,001+ | $500 | 25% off |

#### 1.4.4 Usage Caps and Alerts

| Threshold | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 50% of estimated usage | Email notification |
| 75% of estimated usage | Email + in-app notification |
| 90% of estimated usage | Email + in-app + account manager |
| 100% of estimated usage | Soft limit (continues with overage) |
| 150% of estimated usage | Hard limit (requires authorization) |

### 1.5 Enterprise Agreement

#### 1.5.1 Definition

An Enterprise Agreement (EA) is a customized contract for large organizations requiring flexibility, volume discounts, and tailored terms.

#### 1.5.2 Enterprise Agreement Eligibility

| Criteria | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| Annual Commitment | $500,000+ |
| Contract Term | 2+ years |
| Facilities/Entities | 10+ |
| Strategic Value | High (mutual business benefit) |

#### 1.5.3 Enterprise Agreement Benefits

- **Volume Discounts**: 20-40% based on commitment
- **Flexible Deployment**: Mix of SaaS, hybrid, on-premise
- **Custom Terms**: Tailored SLAs, liability, indemnification
- **Growth Provisions**: Pre-negotiated expansion rates
- **Success Resources**: Dedicated TAM, executive sponsor
- **Custom Development**: Option for platform customization

### 1.6 OEM/Embedded License

#### 1.6.1 Definition

An OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) license allows partners to embed GreenLang technology into their own products for resale.

#### 1.6.2 OEM License Models

| Model | Description | Pricing |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Per-Unit** | Fixed fee per end-customer deployment | $10,000 - $50,000 per unit |
| **Revenue Share** | Percentage of partner revenue | 15-30% of related revenue |
| **Flat Fee** | Annual fee for unlimited deployment | $500,000 - $2,000,000/year |
| **Hybrid** | Base fee + per-unit or revenue share | Negotiated |

#### 1.6.3 OEM Requirements

- Minimum annual commitment: $250,000
- Partner certification required
- Joint marketing obligations
- Technical integration review
- Annual business reviews

## 2. License Terms and Conditions

### 2.1 Grant of License

#### 2.1.1 Standard License Grant

> Subject to the terms of this Agreement and payment of applicable fees, GreenLang grants Customer a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Software during the License Term solely for Customer's internal business purposes.

#### 2.1.2 Scope of Use

| Use Right | Subscription | Perpetual | Usage-Based |
|-----------|:------------:|:---------:|:-----------:|
| Internal business use | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Use for customer-facing services | Limited | Limited | Yes |
| Subsidiary use | Per entity | Per entity | Per entity |
| Affiliate use | Additional license | Additional license | Included |
| Contractor access | Yes (as users) | Yes (as users) | Yes (as MAU) |

### 2.2 License Restrictions

#### 2.2.1 Prohibited Activities

The Customer shall NOT:

1. **Reverse Engineer**: Decompile, disassemble, or reverse engineer the Software
2. **Modify**: Create derivative works or modify the Software
3. **Distribute**: Sublicense, sell, resell, or distribute the Software
4. **Compete**: Use the Software to develop competing products
5. **Benchmark**: Publish benchmark results without consent
6. **Transfer**: Assign or transfer the license without consent
7. **Circumvent**: Bypass security or licensing controls
8. **Misuse Data**: Use data for purposes beyond licensed scope

#### 2.2.2 Violation Consequences

| Violation Severity | First Occurrence | Repeat Occurrence |
|-------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| Minor | Written warning | 30-day cure period |
| Moderate | 30-day cure period | License suspension |
| Severe | Immediate suspension | License termination |
| Criminal | Immediate termination | Legal action |

### 2.3 Intellectual Property

#### 2.3.1 Ownership

- **GreenLang IP**: All rights to the Software, including patents, copyrights, trade secrets, and trademarks remain with GreenLang
- **Customer Data**: Customer retains ownership of all data input into the Software
- **Derived Insights**: Anonymized, aggregated insights may be used by GreenLang for improvement
- **Customizations**: Custom development ownership per statement of work

#### 2.3.2 Feedback and Improvements

Any feedback, suggestions, or improvements proposed by Customer become GreenLang property without compensation, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

### 2.4 Data Rights

#### 2.4.1 Customer Data Rights

- Full ownership of input data
- Right to export data at any time
- Right to deletion upon termination
- Right to data portability (standard formats)

#### 2.4.2 GreenLang Data Rights

- Right to process data for service delivery
- Right to create anonymized benchmarks
- Right to use aggregated data for ML training
- No right to sell or share identifiable data

## 3. Compliance Requirements

### 3.1 License Compliance

#### 3.1.1 Customer Obligations

Customers must:

1. **Accurate Reporting**: Provide accurate usage data when requested
2. **User Management**: Maintain accurate user counts
3. **Access Control**: Implement appropriate access controls
4. **Audit Cooperation**: Cooperate with compliance audits
5. **Prompt Remediation**: Address compliance issues within 30 days

#### 3.1.2 Compliance Monitoring

| Monitoring Method | Frequency | Customer Visibility |
|-------------------|-----------|---------------------|
| Automated telemetry | Continuous | Usage dashboard |
| Self-certification | Annual | Customer portal |
| License review | Annual (Enterprise) | Joint meeting |
| Formal audit | As needed | Scheduled notice |

### 3.2 Data Compliance

#### 3.2.1 Data Protection Requirements

| Regulation | Requirement | GreenLang Compliance |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| GDPR | Data protection, privacy | Certified, DPA available |
| CCPA | California privacy | Certified, addendum |
| SOC 2 Type II | Security controls | Certified annually |

#### 3.2.2 Data Processing Agreement

All customers processing personal data must execute a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) covering:
- Processing instructions and purposes
- Security measures
- Sub-processor management
- Data subject rights
- Breach notification
- Cross-border transfers

### 3.3 Export Compliance

#### 3.3.1 Export Restrictions

GreenLang Software may be subject to export control laws. Customers agree to:
- Not export to embargoed countries
- Not export to prohibited parties
- Comply with all applicable export laws
- Notify GreenLang of any export inquiries

#### 3.3.2 Restricted Countries

The Software may not be exported to:
- Countries under comprehensive US sanctions
- Entities on denied parties lists
- End-users for prohibited purposes

## 4. Audit Provisions

### 4.1 Audit Rights

#### 4.1.1 GreenLang Audit Rights

GreenLang reserves the right to audit Customer's use of the Software to verify compliance with license terms. Audits may be conducted:

- **Self-Certification**: Customer completes annual compliance questionnaire
- **Remote Audit**: Automated tools verify usage within parameters
- **On-Site Audit**: Physical inspection with 30-day notice
- **Third-Party Audit**: Independent auditor engagement

#### 4.1.2 Audit Triggers

| Trigger | Audit Type | Notice Period |
|---------|------------|---------------|
| Annual compliance | Self-certification | 30 days |
| Usage anomaly detected | Remote audit | 15 days |
| Customer request | Any | Customer initiated |
| Reasonable suspicion | On-site or third-party | 30 days |
| Contract renewal | License review | 60 days |

### 4.2 Audit Process

#### 4.2.1 Pre-Audit

1. **Notice**: GreenLang provides written notice of audit intent
2. **Scope**: Define audit scope and data requirements
3. **Scheduling**: Agree on audit dates (business hours)
4. **Preparation**: Customer prepares required documentation

#### 4.2.2 During Audit

1. **Access**: Provide auditor access to relevant systems and records
2. **Cooperation**: Respond to auditor questions promptly
3. **Documentation**: Provide requested license, user, and usage data
4. **Confidentiality**: Auditor signs NDA for customer data

#### 4.2.3 Post-Audit

1. **Report**: GreenLang provides audit findings within 30 days
2. **Discussion**: Joint review of findings
3. **Remediation**: Address any compliance gaps
4. **Payment**: Pay any underpayment plus true-up fees

### 4.3 Audit Outcomes

#### 4.3.1 Compliance Scenarios

| Finding | Customer Action | GreenLang Action |
|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Fully compliant | None required | Certificate issued |
| Minor variance (<5%) | True-up payment | No penalty |
| Moderate variance (5-15%) | True-up + 10% fee | Warning issued |
| Major variance (15-30%) | True-up + 25% fee | Compliance plan required |
| Severe variance (>30%) | True-up + 50% fee + audit costs | License review |

#### 4.3.2 Dispute Resolution

1. **Good Faith Discussion**: 15-day discussion period
2. **Escalation**: Escalate to executive sponsors
3. **Third-Party Review**: Independent auditor determination
4. **Binding Arbitration**: Final resolution mechanism

### 4.4 Record Keeping

#### 4.4.1 Customer Records

Customers must maintain records for the duration of the license plus 2 years:

- User counts and access logs
- Deployment locations
- Usage metrics
- License keys and certificates
- Payment records

#### 4.4.2 GreenLang Records

GreenLang maintains:

- License grant records
- Payment history
- Support interactions
- Audit history
- Compliance certifications

## 5. License Lifecycle Management

### 5.1 License Activation

#### 5.1.1 Activation Process

1. **Order Execution**: Customer executes order form
2. **Payment**: Initial payment received
3. **Provisioning**: Account and environment setup
4. **Key Generation**: License key or token issued
5. **Activation**: Customer activates license
6. **Verification**: Activation confirmed

#### 5.1.2 License Key Management

| Key Type | Format | Duration | Renewal |
|----------|--------|----------|---------|
| Trial | Time-limited token | 30 days | One extension |
| Subscription | Annual token | 1 year | Auto-renew |
| Perpetual | Permanent key | Indefinite | Maintenance renewal |
| Enterprise | Certificate | Per agreement | Custom |

### 5.2 License Modification

#### 5.2.1 Upgrades

- Available at any time during subscription term
- Prorated fee for upgraded capacity/features
- No credit for unused lower-tier time
- Immediate activation

#### 5.2.2 Downgrades

- Available at renewal only
- 60-day notice required
- Data migration assistance provided
- Feature access restricted at renewal

### 5.3 License Renewal

#### 5.3.1 Renewal Process

| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| -90 days | Renewal notice sent |
| -60 days | Renewal proposal generated |
| -30 days | Final renewal reminder |
| -7 days | Urgent renewal warning |
| 0 days | License expires (grace period begins) |
| +30 days | Grace period ends |

#### 5.3.2 Renewal Pricing

| Scenario | Renewal Price |
|----------|---------------|
| On-time renewal | Current list price or contract rate |
| Late renewal (<30 days) | +5% late fee |
| Lapsed renewal (30-90 days) | +10% + reactivation fee |
| Lapsed renewal (>90 days) | New customer pricing |

### 5.4 License Termination

#### 5.4.1 Termination Scenarios

| Scenario | Process | Data Retention |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| Customer-initiated (convenience) | 60-day notice | 90 days export period |
| Customer-initiated (cause) | Immediate | 90 days export period |
| GreenLang-initiated (cause) | Per violation terms | 30 days minimum |
| Non-payment | 30-day cure, then terminate | 60 days |
| Mutual agreement | As negotiated | As negotiated |

#### 5.4.2 Post-Termination

- All license rights terminate immediately
- Customer must cease all use
- Customer may export data during retention period
- GreenLang deletes customer data after retention
- Confidentiality obligations survive
