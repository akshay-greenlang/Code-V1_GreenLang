## 3. Performance-Based Pricing Model

### 3.1 Overview

Performance-based pricing aligns GreenLang's revenue with customer success, creating shared incentives for optimal outcomes.

### 3.2 Performance Metrics

| Metric | Measurement | Verification | Payment Trigger |
|--------|-------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Energy Savings** | kWh/MMBtu reduced | Utility bills, meters | Quarterly true-up |
| **Emissions Reduction** | tCO2e avoided | Calculations, audits | Annual verification |
| **Compliance Completion** | Reports submitted | Registry confirmation | Per submission |
| **Uptime Improvement** | Reduced downtime | System logs | Monthly measurement |
| **Cost Avoidance** | Penalties avoided | Audit records | Annual review |

### 3.3 Performance-Based Pricing Structure

#### Option A: Gain-Share Model

| Savings Range | Customer Share | GreenLang Share |
|--------------|----------------|-----------------|
| First $100K | 90% | 10% |
| $100K - $500K | 85% | 15% |
| $500K - $1M | 80% | 20% |
| $1M - $5M | 75% | 25% |
| $5M+ | 70% | 30% |

**Example:**
- Customer achieves $2M in energy savings
- First $100K: Customer $90K, GreenLang $10K
- Next $400K: Customer $340K, GreenLang $60K
- Next $500K: Customer $400K, GreenLang $100K
- Next $1M: Customer $750K, GreenLang $250K
- **Totals: Customer $1.58M (79%), GreenLang $420K (21%)**

#### Option B: Guaranteed Savings Model

| Commitment Level | Base Fee Reduction | Performance Guarantee |
|-----------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Conservative | 0% | 10% savings guaranteed |
| Standard | 10% | 15% savings guaranteed |
| Aggressive | 20% | 20% savings guaranteed |

**Risk Mitigation:**
- If savings fall short, GreenLang credits the difference
- Maximum credit: 50% of annual fees
- Measurement methodology pre-agreed

#### Option C: Outcome-Based Pricing

| Outcome | Fixed Price | Success Metric |
|---------|------------|----------------|
| CSRD Compliance | $200K | Audit-approved submission |
| 20% Energy Reduction | $300K | Verified via utility bills |
| Net Zero Roadmap | $150K | Science-based target approval |
| ISO 50001 Certification | $100K | Certification achieved |

### 3.4 Performance Pricing Terms

- **Baseline Period**: 12 months prior to implementation
- **Measurement Period**: Minimum 12 months post-implementation
- **Verification**: Third-party verification for claims >$500K
- **Payment Terms**: Quarterly true-up or annual settlement
- **Adjustment Factors**: Weather normalization, production changes

## 6. ROI Calculator Methodology

### 6.1 ROI Calculation Framework

```
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs x 100%

Where:
- Total Benefits = Direct Savings + Indirect Benefits + Risk Avoidance
- Total Costs = License + Implementation + Training + Ongoing Operations
```

### 6.2 Benefit Categories

#### 6.2.1 Direct Savings

| Category | Calculation Method | Typical Range |
|----------|-------------------|---------------|
| Energy Cost Reduction | Baseline kWh x % reduction x $/kWh | 15-30% |
| Labor Productivity | Hours saved x hourly cost | 50-80% time reduction |
| Consultant Replacement | Previous consultant spend | 80-90% reduction |
| Compliance Penalties Avoided | Penalty risk x probability | 100% avoidance |
| Carbon Credit Savings | tCO2e x $/tCO2e x reduction % | 20-40% reduction |

#### 6.2.2 Indirect Benefits

| Category | Calculation Method | Typical Value |
|----------|-------------------|---------------|
| Revenue from Sustainability | New contracts won | 5-15% revenue lift |
| Brand Value Improvement | Market cap increase | 2-5% premium |
| Employee Retention | Reduced turnover cost | 10-20% improvement |
| Faster Time-to-Compliance | Avoided delays | 3-6 months faster |
| Competitive Advantage | Market share gain | Varies |

#### 6.2.3 Risk Avoidance

| Risk Category | Calculation Method | Typical Value |
|---------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Regulatory Fines | Maximum fine x probability | $1M-$50M avoided |
| Market Access Loss | Revenue at risk x probability | 10-100% of EU revenue |
| Reputational Damage | Crisis cost estimate | $5M-$100M avoided |
| Supply Chain Disruption | Business interruption cost | $1M-$20M avoided |
| Stranded Assets | Asset writedown risk | Varies by industry |

### 6.3 Cost Categories

| Category | Calculation | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| License Fees | Annual subscription cost | Fixed |
| Implementation | Professional services | One-time, amortized |
| Training | Initial + ongoing training | Annual allocation |
| Internal Resources | FTE allocation x cost | Typically 0.25-1 FTE |
| Change Management | Communication, adoption | Typically 5-10% of project |

## 7. TCO Calculator Approach

### 7.1 TCO Framework

```
TCO = Direct Costs + Indirect Costs + Hidden Costs + Opportunity Costs

Components:
1. Direct Costs
   - License/Subscription fees
   - Implementation services
   - Training costs
   - Integration development

2. Indirect Costs
   - Internal staff time
   - Infrastructure (if on-prem)
   - Change management

3. Hidden Costs
   - Learning curve productivity loss
   - Technical debt
   - Customization maintenance

4. Opportunity Costs
   - Delayed time-to-value
   - Features not utilized
```

### 8.2 Metering Technical Specifications

```yaml
# Metering Configuration
metering:
  collection_interval: 60  # seconds
  aggregation_interval: 3600  # hourly rollup
  retention_raw: 7  # days
  retention_aggregated: 365  # days

  dimensions:
    - name: users
      type: gauge
      measurement: active_sessions

    - name: data_points
      type: counter
      measurement: ingestion_count

    - name: api_calls
      type: counter
      measurement: request_count
      tags:
        - endpoint
        - method
        - status_code

    - name: agent_invocations
      type: counter
      measurement: execution_count
      tags:
        - agent_type
        - agent_id
        - status

    - name: storage
      type: gauge
      measurement: bytes_stored
      aggregation: daily_average

    - name: compute
      type: counter
      measurement: compute_seconds
      tags:
        - resource_type
        - model_type
```
