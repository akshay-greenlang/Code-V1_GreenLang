## Executive Summary

GreenLang offers three distinct edition tiers designed to meet the needs of organizations at every stage of their climate intelligence journey. This Good/Better/Best framework ensures customers can start with essential capabilities and upgrade as their requirements grow.

### 1.2 Value Proposition by Tier

#### Essentials (Good) - "Start Strong"
> Perfect for organizations beginning their sustainability journey. Get compliant fast with core monitoring and basic optimization capabilities.

**Key Benefits:**
- Achieve regulatory compliance within 30 days
- Reduce manual reporting effort by 80%
- Establish baseline metrics and tracking
- Access 25 specialized AI agents
- Standard integrations and support

#### Professional (Better) - "Scale Smart"
> Designed for growing organizations ready to leverage advanced ML capabilities for significant cost savings and comprehensive compliance.

**Key Benefits:**
- 20-30% energy cost reduction through ML optimization
- Advanced predictive analytics and forecasting
- Multi-regulation compliance (CSRD, CBAM, ISO)
- 100 specialized AI agents
- Priority support and dedicated success manager

#### Enterprise (Best) - "Lead Confidently"
> The complete climate intelligence platform for organizations demanding the highest performance, customization, and global capabilities.

**Key Benefits:**
- Full AI agent library (500+ agents)
- Custom agent creation via Agent Factory
- Unlimited users, facilities, and data
- 24/7 priority support with dedicated TAM
- White-label and on-premise options

## 4. Upgrade Paths

### 4.1 Essentials to Professional

**Trigger Points:**
- Exceeding 100,000 data points/month
- Need for ML-powered optimization
- Multi-regulation compliance requirements
- More than 10 users needed
- Advanced analytics requirements

**Migration Process:**
1. Contact Customer Success Manager
2. Needs assessment (1 week)
3. Configuration migration (1-2 weeks)
4. Feature activation (same day)
5. Training on new capabilities (1 week)

**Upgrade Incentives:**
- 10% discount if upgrading within first year
- Prorated billing for current period
- Free implementation support
- 30-day extended trial of Enterprise features

### 4.2 Professional to Enterprise

**Trigger Points:**
- Need for custom agent development
- Enterprise security requirements (VPC, SSO)
- Global deployment needs
- On-premise requirements
- White-label needs
- More than 50 users

**Migration Process:**
1. Executive sponsor alignment
2. Technical architecture review (2 weeks)
3. Security and compliance assessment (1 week)
4. Migration planning (2 weeks)
5. Phased rollout (4-8 weeks)
6. Hypercare support (30 days)

**Upgrade Incentives:**
- Multi-year discount (10% for 2 years, 15% for 3 years)
- Custom implementation included
- Agent Factory training (40 hours)
- Executive business reviews

### 5.3 Quick Assessment Questionnaire

Answer these questions to determine your ideal tier:

1. **How many users need access?**
   - 1-10: Essentials
   - 11-50: Professional
   - 50+: Enterprise

2. **How many facilities/locations?**
   - 1-5: Essentials
   - 6-25: Professional
   - 25+: Enterprise

3. **Do you need ML-powered optimization?**
   - No: Essentials
   - Yes: Professional or Enterprise

4. **Do you need custom agent development?**
   - No: Essentials or Professional
   - Yes: Enterprise

5. **What are your security requirements?**
   - Standard: Essentials or Professional
   - Enhanced (VPC, dedicated): Enterprise

6. **Deployment preference?**
   - SaaS only: Any tier
   - Hybrid: Professional or Enterprise
   - On-premise: Enterprise only
