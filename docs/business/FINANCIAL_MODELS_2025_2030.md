## Three Strategic Scenarios

### SCENARIO A: Application-First Strategy
**Thesis:** Build vertical SaaS applications for specific compliance needs (CBAM, CSRD, Taxonomy, EUDR). Land enterprise customers quickly with complete solutions.

**Expected Outcome:** Faster revenue, proven PMF, but limited platform leverage

### SCENARIO B: Platform-First Strategy
**Thesis:** Build horizontal developer platform for climate agents/packs. Enable ecosystem of developers building on GreenLang infrastructure.

**Expected Outcome:** Slower initial revenue, but massive platform leverage if successful

### SCENARIO C: Hybrid Strategy (Apps → Platform)
**Thesis:** Start with high-value applications to prove value and generate cash, then expand to platform play with proven product-market fit.

**Expected Outcome:** Balanced risk/reward, staged capital requirements

**Focus:** Build 4-6 vertical compliance applications:

- Direct sales to Fortune 1000 enterprises

- Tier 1 (SMB): $25K-$50K/year (1-2 applications)

- Training: $10K-$25K
