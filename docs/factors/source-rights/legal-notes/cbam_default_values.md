## Notes

* CBAM default values are regulatory annex content of an EU
  Implementing Regulation. Decision 2011/833/EU establishes the EU
  reuse policy; redistribution is allowed with attribution.
* These defaults are pinned to Reg 2023/1773 Annex IV (current
  effective period). Watch for subsequent implementing acts that
  revise Annex IV.

## Re-approval triggers

Re-confirm on any DG TAXUD revision to Annex IV defaults.
