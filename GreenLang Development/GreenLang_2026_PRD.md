# PART I: EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

## 1. Vision Statement

**GreenLang** is the world's first **Agentic Climate Operating System** - a runtime + domain-specific language (DSL) that compiles problem specifications into deterministic multi-agent graphs where each specialist agent performs one job extremely well.

### The GreenLang Promise
> "From complex climate challenges to audit-ready outputs in days, not months, with zero hallucination and complete reproducibility."
