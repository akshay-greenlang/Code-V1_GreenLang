# 1. CLIMATE OS VISION & MISSION

## The Problem: $50 Trillion Climate Transition Has No Operating System

The global economy faces the largest transformation in human history: a $50+ trillion transition to net zero by 2050. Every company on Earth must measure, reduce, report, and adapt to climate change. Today, this is done with:

- **Consultants**: $500K-$2M per project, 3-6 months, non-scalable
- **Spreadsheets**: Error-prone, non-auditable, non-reproducible
- **Point solutions**: Siloed data, manual reconciliation, no interoperability
- **Generic AI**: Hallucination risk in compliance-critical domains

**GreenLang Climate OS** is the world's first **agentic operating system for climate intelligence** - a platform where thousands of deterministic, zero-hallucination AI agents collaborate to solve every climate challenge across every industry, every regulation, and every geography.

## Mission Statement

> **To build the operating system that powers humanity's transition to a net-zero, climate-resilient economy by making climate intelligence deterministic, affordable, and universally accessible.**
